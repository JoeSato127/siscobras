IISSAC = Indonesian International Small School Activities Conference

Being selected to represent your school in the Indonesian International Small Schools Activities Conference (IISSAC) is an honor that highlights a student's dedication, skill, and character. It signifies not only athletic or academic talent but also the ability to embody teamwork, sportsmanship, and leadership. Students chosen for these teams serve as ambassadors of their school's values, representing their peers on a larger stage, and fostering intercultural friendships.
